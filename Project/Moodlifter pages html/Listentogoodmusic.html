Listen to good music

Every little thing matters. That's why
even if it sounds like it won't make a
tremendous difference, listening to your
favorite music can have a powerful effect on
your anxiety. They key is to not just choose
songs you like, however. The key is also to
make sure you're listening to music that
represents the way you want to feel. Happy or
relaxing music, not just any music.

The reality is that music does affect emotions.
So while many people find it soothing to listen
to angry music when you're angry or sad music
when you're sad, the truth is that this type of
music will only help you get in touch with those
negative emotions. They won't help you feel better.
When you're trying to stop anxiety now, you should
listen to music that will help you feel the way you
want to feel.
