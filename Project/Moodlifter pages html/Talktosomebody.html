Talk to Somebody

Effective anxiety reduction is often
about distraction, since your mind can
be your worst enemy when you have severe
anxiety symptoms. A very effective
technique is to talk to someone you like
and trust, especially on the phone. Don't
be shy about your anxiety,tell them you
feel anxious and explain what you're feeling.
Talking to nice people keeps your mind off
of your symptoms, and the supportive nature
of friends and family gives you that added
boost of confidence. If you're suffering from
a panic attack, it also helps you feel more
confident that if something was wrong, you'd
have someone that can watch over you.
