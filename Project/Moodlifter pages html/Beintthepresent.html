Be in the present

Finally, simply learning to live for today
can impact your anxiety. One of the most
important things that psychologists and
counselors teach those with anxiety is:
"Okay, you're anxious. So what?"

Those with anxiety often start to focus too
much on how they feel and their worries about
the future. Each day becomes trying to live with
anxiety instead of trying to live in general.
Learning to embrace the idea that you have anxiety
and trying to live a great and exciting life anyway
is important.

And what's interesting is that if you can learn to
finally have that mindset to let yourself experience
the fear and try to live the life anyway - you will find
that your anxiety tends to dissipate with it. It's not a
cure, but it's close.
